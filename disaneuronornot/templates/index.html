Welcome to DIS A NEURON or NOT

The goal is for you to identify whether the displayed image looks like a neuron or not. This will help train a simple convolutional neural network to recognize whether the pattern provided as input is a neuron or not. Hence the name, DIS A NEURON.

You can select whether you think it is a neuron or not by clicking on the two buttons right of the image. Otherwise you can also press keys 'y' if you think it is a neuron, or 'n' if you think it is not a neuron.
You can go back once to the previous image if you think you classified wrongly, by clicking on the button left of the picture. You can also press key 'd' to do that.
Have fun playing!
